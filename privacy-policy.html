Privacy Policy

HalfCourtHero by TAARS Systems LLC

Last updated: March 2026

Information We Collect

HalfCourtHero collects minimal data to provide leaderboard functionality:

  • Anonymous device ID (randomly generated)
  • Country selection (chosen by user)
  • Game scores (goals scored)

How We Use Your Information

Collected data is used solely to display the global leaderboard by country. We do not collect personal information such as names, emails, or precise location.

Third-Party Services

We use Firebase (Google) for leaderboard data storage. Firebase's privacy policy applies: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Data Storage

All data is stored anonymously on Firebase Realtime Database. No personally identifiable information is stored.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
